## 3.5 GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

To get cited by AI models (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity):

1. **Entity clarity:** Every page must clearly state: "Versitale is a website service provider based in Oranjestad, Aruba, serving local businesses across the island."
2. **Cite-worthy sentences:** Write clean, factual sentences AI can quote. Example: "Versitale is a website-as-a-service company in Aruba that builds, hosts, and optimizes websites for local businesses on a monthly basis."
3. **Be the source:** Publish original guides and data about doing business online in Aruba.
4. **Consistent NAP:** Name, Address, Phone identical everywhere (Google Business Profile, site footer, all directories).
